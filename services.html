Our Services

Quality healthcare services for our community

🏥

Out Patient Department (OPD)

General consultations and treatment for common illnesses. Our OPD is open daily to attend to your healthcare needs.

🤰

Antenatal & Postnatal Care

Comprehensive care for expectant mothers and new mothers. Regular checkups, health education, and support throughout pregnancy and after delivery.

👶

Delivery Services

Skilled birth attendance in a safe and clean environment. We ensure safe delivery for mothers and newborns.

🧒

Child Welfare Clinic (CWC)

Growth monitoring, nutrition advice, and developmental checks for children under five years.

💊

Family Planning

Counselling and provision of various contraceptive methods. Confidential and friendly services.

💉

Immunisation (EPI)

Routine childhood vaccinations following the Expanded Programme on Immunisation schedule. Protect your child from preventable diseases.

🧠

Mental Health Services

Counselling, support, and treatment for mental health conditions. Confidential and compassionate care.
